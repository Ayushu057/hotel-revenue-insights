# 📊 Business Insights

## 🔹 Revenue Insights

* Mumbai generates the highest revenue among all cities.
* Revenue contribution is concentrated in a few key locations.
* Certain hotels consistently outperform others in revenue generation.

## 🔹 Occupancy Insights

* Occupancy rates are higher during weekends compared to weekdays.
* Some cities show stable occupancy trends, while others fluctuate significantly.

## 🔹 Booking Trends

* A significant portion of bookings gets cancelled, impacting overall revenue.
* Booking patterns indicate seasonal demand variations.

## 🔹 Performance Insights

* High revenue does not always translate to high profit.
* Some properties have lower efficiency despite high booking volumes.

## 🔹 Key Observations

* Demand is uneven across locations.
* Customer behavior impacts revenue consistency.
* Strategic pricing and capacity planning can improve performance.

## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis highlights the importance of location-based strategy, demand forecasting, and cancellation management to improve overall business performance.
